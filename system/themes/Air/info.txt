Air theme
=========

This theme is based on two other themes:

Gtk2: elementary. Designed and developed by Daniel Foré <Daniel.p.Fore@gmail.com>
Gtk3: Greybird. Designed and developed by Simon Steinbeiß and Satyajit Sahoo <satyajit.happy@gmail.com>

The Theme consists only Gtk2 and Gtk3. If you want it for metacity, unity and xfwm4
please download the original package from the following links and copy them into 
your ~/.themes directory:

elementary: http://danrabbit.deviantart.com/art/elementary-gtk-theme-83104033
Greybird: http://shimmerproject.org/projects/greybird/

License: both GPL3

Please do not report bugs to them. Instead send an email to <fvwmnightshade@gmail.com>

Thanks
