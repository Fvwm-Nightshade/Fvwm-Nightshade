CONTACT
-------
If you have problems or want to contribute in any way three possibilities 
are available at the moment:

Mailing Lists
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/fvwmnightshade[fvwm.nightshade] (Discussion and questions list)  

https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/fvwmnightshade-workers[fvwm.nightshade.workers] (Beta testers and contributors list)

Bug / Feature Request Tracker
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
https://github.com/Fvwm-Nightshade/Fvwm-Nightshade/issues[Here] you can enter bugs or feature requests if needed.

Or send us an email to <fvwmnightshade@gmail.com>. We would be happy to 
hear from you!
